Recipe

  1. Slice onion into rings
  2. Slice the tomato into thin slices.
  3. Cut the letuce so you have individual leaves.
  4. Heat some olive oil in a pan at a high heat.
  5. Put the burgers onto the pan and turn every few minutes untill cooked.
  6. Toast the burger buns.
  7. Put Burger, Ketchup, Onions, Lettuce, and Tomato on the toasted buns.
  8. Eat
